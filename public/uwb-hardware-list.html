UWB-Based Lap Tracker: Final Hardware List

Main Controller

  • Vilros Raspberry Pi 4 Complete Starter Kit
    Includes Pi 4 Board (8GB), fan-cooled case, 64GB microSD, power supply, HDMI, and more
    Price: Included in kit

UWB Modules

  • 7x DWM1001-DEV Modules (Qorvo)
    Used as 4 anchors, 1 tag, 1 gateway (Pi), and 1 spare
    Price: $251.07 total (~$35.87 per unit)

Display

  • HAMTYSAN 10.1" Touchscreen Monitor
    Resolution: 1024x600, HDMI/USB
    Price: Varies (~$75–$95)

Power Solutions

  • Qimoo 3.7V 200mAh LiPo Battery (Wristband Tag)
    With JST PH2.0mm connector
  • MakerFocus 3.7V 2000mAh LiPo Batteries (Anchors)
    With Micro JST 1.25 plugs and protection board — 4 units
  • TP4056 Charging Boards
    Micro USB, 1A, 10-pack — for anchor/tag recharging
  • Anker PowerCore 10,000mAh Power Bank
    Used for Raspberry Pi / display power

Accessories

  • Geekworm Mounting Kit (standoffs, screws)
  • Freenove Raspberry Pi Ultimate Starter Kit (components + sensors)
  • Letool JST 1.25mm Connectors (30 pairs, male/female)
  • Heat Shrink Tubing Kit (580 pcs)
  • YIHUA Soldering Iron Station + Extras
  • Stainless Steel Soldering Flux Paste
  • Dupont Jumper Wires (Male/Female/Mix sets)

To Be Purchased Locally

  • Plastic enclosures for anchors
  • Wristband case or housing for swimmer tag
  • Labeling/numbering for easy field setup

Project Notes

  • 1 Pi + touchscreen will act as main controller and gateway.
  • 4 anchors arranged in X formation outside pool lanes.
  • 1 wearable wristband tag per swimmer.
  • All modules are battery-powered for portability.
  • System designed to be field-ready and app-compatible in future iterations.
